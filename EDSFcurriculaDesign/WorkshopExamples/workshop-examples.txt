This directory contains examples used for practical exercises with the EDSF Toolkit

1) Examples of CV collected from Internet and primarily from indeed.com
Collection is provided for different intended professions or positions (e.g. Data Scientist, Data Analyst, Data Steward, others)

2) Examples of using EDSF by the EDISON Champion universities for developing their 
Data Science programmes (based on the EDISON Deliverable D3.3)  

3) Example Data Science competences self assessment questions
